## Business Overview

Soleil Infusion is an IV infusion wellness bar operating in Glen Burnie, Maryland, serving a hybrid medical-lifestyle market. Founded and operated by Thuy Cao, the business provides premium intravenous therapy sessions in a boutique clinic setting, partnering with Voshell's Pharmacy for compounded sterile IV preparations.

**Primary Customer Segments:**
- Korean-American community aged 35-65 (core market)
- First responders and healthcare workers
- BWI airport travelers
- Busy executives and high-performance professionals

**Services & Pricing:**
| Service Tier | Price Range |
|---|---|
| Essential Hydration | $165 – $175 |
| Energy / Immunity | $195 – $215 |
| Performance / Recovery | $225 – $245 |
| Advanced Functional (NAD+, Liver Support) | $275 – $295 |
| Membership (2-4 visits/month) | 10–15% discount |

**Launch Offer:** Buy 2 infusions, get 1 free (pre-launch presale package)

**Pharmacy Partner:** Voshell's Pharmacy (supplies compounded sterile IV preparations)
