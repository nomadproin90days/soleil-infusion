About Soleil Infusion

Soleil Infusion is a forthcoming IV infusion wellness bar converting an underperforming pharmacy space at 801 Landmark Drive, Glen Burnie, Maryland into a 900 sq ft, five-chair infusion bar. Prepared by Thuy Cao (owner), the business is vertically integrated with Voshell's Pharmacy, which supplies compounded sterile IV preparations at wholesale. The two entities are legally separate to comply with anti-kickback statutes.

The core strategy is a Korean-First go-to-market targeting Korean-Americans aged 35-65, for whom IV therapy is a familiar wellness practice — a currently unserved blue ocean market in the region. TAM estimated at $1.2M – $2.5M in Year 1.

03 — Compliance Rules

HIPAA + TCPA Requirements

All GHL automations must comply with HIPAA and TCPA. These rules apply to every SMS and email created in any workflow.

Non-Negotiable Rules
  • NEVER include medication names, compound names, drug names, or specific treatment formulas in any automated message
  • NEVER include diagnosis, symptoms, or any PHI (protected health information) in SMS or email content
  • Refer to all services generically: "your appointment," "your session," "your visit," "your treatment"
  • All SMS workflows must have Stop-on-Response enabled
  • First automated SMS must include: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
  • No SMS before 8:00 AM or after 9:00 PM Eastern Time (Maryland)
  • B2B partner contacts require separate consent tracking from patient contacts
  • All new workflows must be saved as Draft first for review before publishing
