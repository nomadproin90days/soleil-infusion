# WF-01: B2B Lead Nurture & Onboarding

Triggers on tag `b2b-lead` or form submission and delivers a welcome SMS + email, creates a pipeline opportunity, and assigns timed follow-up tasks at 1, 3, and 7 days to onboard business-to-business referral partners (clinics, first responder groups, corporate accounts) into the Soleil Infusion partner network.
