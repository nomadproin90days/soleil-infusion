What It Does & Why It Exists

When an appointment is marked "No Show" in GHL, this workflow tags the contact, waits 30 minutes, and sends a warm rescheduling SMS with a booking link. Two days later, it sends a reschedule offer email. It then checks whether the patient has rebooked — if yes, it swaps tags from no-show to rescheduled; if no, it creates a manual call task for the team.

Research shows that 30-40% of no-shows will reschedule when contacted the same day. Without automation, clinic staff often fail to follow up until it's too late, and the revenue is permanently lost.

Benefits & Outcomes
  • Patients are reached within 30 minutes — while the missed appointment is still top of mind
  • Warm, non-judgmental tone maintains the relationship
  • 2-day email follow-up catches patients who missed or ignored the initial SMS
  • Rescheduled patients re-enter WF-04 automatically via new booking trigger
  • Manual call task ensures persistent no-shows still receive human outreach
Automation Steps
Step 1 — Tag: no-show
Applied immediately when appointment status = No Show.
Wait 30 Minutes
Brief delay allows staff to verify the no-show status before automated outreach begins.
Step 2 — Reschedule SMS
"Hi [Name], we missed you today! Would you like to reschedule? [booking link]. Reply STOP to unsubscribe." Stop-on-response active.
Wait 2 Days → Reschedule Email
Reschedule offer email with booking link, warm tone, and simple call to action.
Condition: Has contact rescheduled?
System checks if new appointment was booked.
Branch A — Rescheduled
Remove no-show tag, apply rescheduled tag. WF-04 fires on new booking automatically.
Branch B — Still No-Show
Manual call task assigned to team: "No-show recovery — phone outreach needed."
