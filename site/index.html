What This System Does

This GoHighLevel automation suite covers every stage of the Soleil Infusion patient and partner lifecycle. Leads from the website, phone, and B2B referral channels are immediately captured and nurtured. Appointment bookings trigger a full pre-visit sequence. Post-visit workflows drive rebooking, Google reviews, and loyalty. Memberships are onboarded and renewed automatically.

All workflows are HIPAA-compliant (no medication names or PHI in messages) and TCPA-compliant (stop-on-response active, time-window enforcement, opt-out language included).
