B2B Lead
Nurture & Onboarding

Welcome, pipeline opportunity creation, and a 7-day task sequence for new business-to-business partner leads.

Trigger
Tag: b2b-lead
Or B2B form submission
Pipeline
B2B Partner Pipeline
Stage: New Lead
Compliance
HIPAA + TCPA
Stop-on-response active
What It Does & Why It Exists

This workflow activates the moment a B2B partner lead enters the system — either via form submission or manual tag application. It delivers an immediate branded welcome via SMS and email, creates a pipeline opportunity in the B2B Partner Pipeline, and assigns a structured sequence of follow-up tasks at 1 day, 3 days, and 7 days.

B2B partners (clinics, first responder organizations, corporate wellness accounts, Korean community networks) represent Soleil Infusion's highest-value referral source. This workflow ensures every B2B lead receives a professional, timely response regardless of team bandwidth.

Business Problem It Solves

Without automation, a busy owner or small team will inevitably miss follow-up windows, lose momentum, and forfeit partnerships that could each generate dozens of patient referrals. Every B2B lead now receives a response within minutes, not hours or days.

Benefits & Outcomes
  • Every B2B lead receives a response within 60 seconds of entering the system
  • Three structured touch points (1d, 3d, 7d) prevent leads from going cold
  • Pipeline opportunity creation gives management real-time B2B funnel visibility
  • Task assignment creates team accountability without micromanagement
  • Professional communication establishes credibility before any human interaction
Automation Steps
Step 1 — Welcome SMS
Immediate. "Hi [Name], welcome to the Soleil Infusion partner network! We're excited to connect. A team member will reach out shortly. Reply STOP to unsubscribe." Stop-on-response active.
Step 2 — Welcome Email
Immediate. Branded welcome email with partnership overview, next steps, and team contact information.
Step 3 — Create Pipeline Opportunity
B2B Partner Pipeline, Stage: New Lead. Immediate pipeline visibility.
Step 4 — Task: 1-Day Follow-up
Assigned to team. Due within 1 business day.
Step 5 — Wait 3 Days → Task
3-day check-in task created for team member.
Step 6 — Wait 7 Days → Task
7-day activation verification task. Final structured touch point.
Step 7 — Tag Contact
Applies b2b-lead tag for segmentation and reporting.
Inputs & Outputs
Inputs
  • Tag: b2b-lead applied to contact
  • OR: B2B lead form submission
  • Contact fields: first name, email, phone, company
Outputs
  • Welcome SMS (immediate)
  • Welcome email (immediate)
  • Pipeline opportunity: B2B Partner Pipeline / New Lead
  • 3 timed follow-up tasks
  • Tag: b2b-lead
Dependencies
  • Pipeline: B2B Partner Pipeline with "New Lead" stage must exist
  • Tags: b2b-lead
  • Compliance: HIPAA — no medical information in messages; TCPA — stop-on-response, opt-out language, 8am-9pm ET window
