What It Does & Why It Exists

This time-critical workflow activates on the presale-lead tag or a presale landing page form submission. It immediately sends the Buy 2 Get 1 Free offer via SMS and a full-details email, creates an opportunity in the Pre-Launch Sales Pipeline, and follows up over 5 days with scarcity-driven messages for non-responders.

Pre-launch package sales allow Thuy Cao to generate revenue before the doors open, validate demand, and build a committed customer base from day one. This workflow automates the entire presale funnel so she can run a presale campaign without a dedicated sales team.

Business Problem It Solves

IV infusion bars are capital-intensive to open. Without pre-launch revenue, operators are entirely dependent on opening-day foot traffic. The Buy 2 Get 1 Free package ($330-$590 value) is a compelling enough offer that motivated early adopters will purchase before the clinic opens — if they are reached and followed up with systematically.

Benefits & Outcomes
  • Immediate offer delivery captures leads while intent is highest
  • Scarcity messaging at 2 and 5 days applies urgency without being pushy
  • Pipeline tracking provides real-time visibility into presale conversion rates
  • Pre-committed customers become the foundation of the membership program
  • Target: 20-50 presale packages = $6,600-$29,500 pre-launch revenue
Automation Steps
Step 1 — Offer SMS
Immediate. "Hi [Name], thanks for your interest in Soleil Infusion! We're offering our launch special — 2 sessions + 1 free — for a limited time. Ready to reserve yours? Reply STOP to unsubscribe."
Step 2 — Full-Details Email
Immediate. Package value, service descriptions, pricing ($165-295/session), booking instructions, and launch timeline.
Step 3 — Pipeline Opportunity
Pre-Launch Sales Pipeline, Stage: New Inquiry. Immediate tracking.
Wait 2 Days → Scarcity SMS
If no reply: "Hi [Name], spots are filling up for our Soleil Infusion launch special! Secure your 3-session package before they're gone."
Wait 3 More Days → Final Email
If no booking: Final follow-up email with social proof, testimonials when available, and direct booking link.
Move to "Offer Sent" Stage
Pipeline updated. Tag presale-offer-sent applied.
