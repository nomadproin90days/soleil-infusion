What It Does & Why It Exists

This dual-trigger workflow handles both ends of the membership lifecycle. The enrollment branch fires on the member-new tag — delivering a welcome SMS, membership email, creating a pipeline opportunity, and tagging the contact as member-active. The renewal branch fires 7 days before the membership_renewal_date custom field date — sending friendly reminders and escalating through SMS and email to prevent churn. Lapsed members are flagged for personal outreach.

Business Problem It Solves

Memberships are Soleil Infusion's most valuable recurring revenue stream. A 2-4 visit/month membership generates $1,500-$3,500+ per member annually. Churn prevention is critical — losing even one member to poor renewal communication represents significant recurring revenue loss. This workflow automates the entire retention sequence without requiring staff to track renewal dates manually.

Enrollment Sequence (Trigger A)
Step 1 — Welcome SMS
"Welcome to Soleil Infusion, [Name]! Your membership is active. Book your first session here: [link]. Reply STOP to unsubscribe."
Step 2 — Welcome Email
Membership benefits, booking link, concierge contact, and membership terms.
Step 3 — Pipeline Opportunity
Membership Pipeline, Stage: Active Member.
Step 4 — Tag: member-active
Applied for segmentation and downstream automations.
Renewal Sequence (Trigger B)
7-Day Reminder SMS
Friendly, non-pushy. "Hi [Name], your Soleil Infusion membership renews soon. We'd love to keep you as a member!"
Renewal Email
Session usage summary, membership benefits recap, and renewal link.
Wait 3 Days → Condition
Has membership renewed? YES → Thank-you SMS + member-renewed tag. NO → Continue sequence.
2-Day Final Reminder SMS
"Your Soleil Infusion membership expires in 2 days. Renew today to keep your discount."
Still Not Renewed → Lapsed
Tag member-lapsed applied. Personal outreach task assigned to team for win-back.
