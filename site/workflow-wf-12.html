What It Does & Why It Exists

When a patient contact is tagged b2b-referral (and the referring_partner custom field is populated), this workflow sends a personalized welcome SMS acknowledging the referral source, creates a pipeline opportunity in the "Referred Lead" stage, adds an internal note identifying the referring partner, and assigns a task to notify the partner of the successful referral.

Once the appointment is completed, the contact is automatically enrolled in the post-infusion follow-up workflow (WF-05). This creates a complete referral-to-follow-up chain with no manual steps.

Business Problem It Solves

B2B partner referrals are Soleil Infusion's highest-quality leads — they arrive pre-warmed with social proof from the referring organization. But tracking which partners send how many patients is difficult without structured automation. Partner relationships need to be nurtured through acknowledgment and feedback loops. Without tracking, high-performing partners go unrecognized and partnerships stagnate.

Benefits & Outcomes
  • Referred patients receive a personalized welcome that reinforces their connection to the referring partner
  • Every referral is logged in the pipeline for partner performance reporting
  • Internal notes create a permanent audit trail linking patients to referring partners
  • Partner notification task closes the loop — recognized partners send more referrals
  • Automatic enrollment in WF-05 ensures same-quality follow-up as direct bookings
Automation Steps
Step 1 — Personalized Welcome SMS
"Welcome to Soleil Infusion, [Name]! We're glad [Referring Partner] sent you our way. Looking forward to meeting you. Reply STOP to unsubscribe." Stop-on-response active.
Step 2 — Pipeline Opportunity
Patient Rx Fulfillment Pipeline, Stage: Referred Lead. Immediate tracking.
Step 3 — Internal Note
"Referred by [Referring Partner]" — permanent record in GHL contact activity.
Step 4 — Partner Notification Task
"Notify referring partner of successful referral conversion" — assigned to team, due same day.
Step 5 — Post-Visit → WF-05
After appointment completion, contact is automatically enrolled in the Post-Infusion Follow-up & Rebooking workflow.
Dependencies
  • Pipeline: Patient Rx Fulfillment Pipeline with "Referred Lead" stage
  • Custom Fields: referring_partner (Text — must be populated before tag is applied)
  • Workflow: WF-05 (post-infusion follow-up)
  • Tags: b2b-referral
  • Compliance: HIPAA — referring partner name is operational data, not PHI
